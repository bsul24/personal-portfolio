My Services

What web project do you need completed? What would help take you or your business to the next level? I pride myself in being able to meet my clients needs and deliver the results that they expect and deserve. Here's how we get there:

  1. Consultation: I'd like to sit down with you and go over exactly what your vision is. What do you need and what do you want in this project? What is your dream?
  2. Proposal: After we go over the details of the project, I will draft a proposal for you based on our discussion and we can determine what the project will ultimately entail.
  3. Project Build: I will create your project based on the agreed upon proposal over the designated timeframe. During this process I will be in regular communication with you to provide detailed updates on the progress of the project.
  4. Project Delivery: Once the project is completed, I will walk you through everything it entails and then it will be handed off to you.
